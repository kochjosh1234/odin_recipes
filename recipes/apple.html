Apple Crisp/h1>

Ingredients

  • 10 cups apples, peeled, cored, sliced
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 bup butter, melted

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F, butter 8x8 dish
  2. In a mixing bowl, add chopped apples, sugar, and cinnamon
  3. In separate bowl, add topping ingrediets (brown sugar, oats, flour, cinnamon, salt, and butter
  4. Spread toppings over apples in baking dish. Bake 40-50 minutes until golden brown and bubbly
  5. Serve warm and enjoy
