Steak Fajitas

Grilled steak with sauteed onions and peppers in a tortilla

  • 3lb Steak
  • 1 ½ cups Goya Mojo Criollo
  • 1 tsp Goya Adobo with Pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 2 red/green/yellow peppers
  • 1 package tortillas
  • Salsa and Guac

Recipe Instructions

  1. Cut steak into thin strips and apply 1/2 seasoning, grill to desired temp
  2. Cut onions and peppers into strips, saute with 1/2 seasoning until soft in the olive oil
  3. Serve steak and veggies in a tortilla, top with salsa and guac
